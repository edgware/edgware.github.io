Where the digital and physical worlds meet

An ecosystem of the intelligent devices are rapidly changing the way that we live and work in the physical world. Edgware supports smart machine-to-machine (M2M) interaction to accelerate building and integrating those systems.

Services at the edge of the network

Local processing of information at its point of origin, saving valuable network bandwidth and managing the large volumes of data captured where the digital world meets the physical world.

Built on MQTT

With MQTT at its core, Edgware decouples the producers and consumers of information by federating a network of MQTT compatible brokers into a single virtual publish/subscribe bus.
